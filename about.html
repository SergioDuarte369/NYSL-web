The Northside Youth Soccer League aspires to develop strong, well-rouded, and mindful athletes through the building of character, self-discipline and leadership

General Information

The Northside Youth Soccer League was established in 1996 to provide athletes residig in Chicago's Northside neighoods an enviroment in which to learn and play soccer. To be a menber of NYSL, you must be between the ages of 4-12 and resie in a Chicago northside neighborhood. NYSL is ran by a small full-time staff and relies on the generous volunteer time of parents and previous league menbers
